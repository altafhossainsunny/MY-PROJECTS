⚙ How it Works
1
Text Preprocessing
Lowercase, remove non-alpha chars, tokenize sentences & words.
2
Stopword Removal
NLTK English stopwords and punctuation stripped out.
3
Lemmatization
POS-tag every token and lemmatize with WordNetLemmatizer.
4
TF-IDF Vectorization
Top-1000 unigram + bigram features extracted.
5
SVC Prediction
Support Vector Classifier outputs Spam (1) or Ham (0).
🤖 Model Facts
Trained on the UCI SMS Spam Collection — 5,572 labelled messages.
SVC with RBF kernel + TF-IDF (max 1,000 features, 1–2 grams).
Achieves >97% precision on the spam class to minimise false positives.
Result confidence shown as an animated probability bar.
